History of Scissors

Long ago...

The earliest known scissors appeared in Mesopotamia 3,000 years ago. These were of the 'spring scissor' type comprising two bronze blades connected at the handles by a thin, flexible strip of curved bronze which served to hold the blades in alignment, to allow them to be squeezed together, and to pull them apart when released. Pivoted scissors were invented by Romans in 100 AD. This invention is used nowadays to cut many materials and it is not going anywhere.
